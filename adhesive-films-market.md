<p><p>The adhesive films market consists of various types, including pressure-sensitive, hot-melt, light-cured, and others. Pressure-sensitive films bond upon application of light pressure and are widely used in labeling and packaging. Hot-melt films utilize heat for adhesion, providing strong bonds upon cooling, ideal for manufacturing processes. Light-cured films require ultraviolet or visible light for curing, offering quick setting times and strong bonds. Other types may include solvent-based and thermoplastic adhesive films, serving diverse applications across multiple industries.</p></p>
