<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Electric Power Steering (EPS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Electric Power Steering (EPS) systems enhance vehicle control and comfort through electric assistance. There are three main types: </p><p>1. **C-EPS (Column Electric Power Steering)** uses an electric motor mounted on the steering column, providing a compact solution suitable for small vehicles. </p><p>2. **P-EPS (Pinion Electric Power Steering)** employs an electric motor on the pinion gear, offering improved feedback and efficiency for a wider range of vehicles. </p><p>3. **R-EPS (Rack Electric Power Steering)** integrates the motor directly into the steering rack, delivering high performance and enhanced responsiveness for larger vehicles.</p></p>
