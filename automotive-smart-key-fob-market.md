<p><strong>The Automotive Smart Key Fob Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Smart Key Fob market primarily consists of two types: Passive Keyless Entry Systems (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry Systems (RKES). PKES allows drivers to unlock and start their vehicles without physically pressing a button, utilizing proximity sensors to detect key fobs within a certain range. In contrast, RKES requires users to press a button on the fob to unlock or start the vehicle, providing a simple, remote-controlled access method. Both enhance convenience and security for vehicle owners.</p></p>
