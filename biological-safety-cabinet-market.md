<p><p>The Biological Safety Cabinet (BSC) market consists of several types designed for specific containment needs. Class II Type A cabinets provide personnel and environmental protection and can exhaust air back into the room, suitable for low-risk procedures. Class II Type B cabinets exhaust air outside the facility, offering greater protection for volatile substances. Class III cabinets are fully enclosed and offer the highest level of containment, ideal for handling highly infectious materials in a secure environment.</p></p>
