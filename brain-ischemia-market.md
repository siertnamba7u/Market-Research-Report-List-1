<p><p>Brain ischemia market primarily comprises three therapy types: anticoagulation therapy, antiplatelet therapy, and other treatments. Anticoagulation therapy focuses on preventing blood clots, thereby enhancing blood flow to the brain. Antiplatelet therapy aims to inhibit platelet aggregation, reducing the risk of stroke and other ischemic events. Other therapies may include neuroprotective agents and adjunctive treatments that support brain health. Together, these therapies address varying aspects of brain ischemia, offering diverse treatment options for affected patients.</p></p>
