<p><p>The Cell-Free Protein Expression Kit market comprises three main types based on their expression systems. Cell Extract Based kits utilize lysates from cells, offering flexibility and rapid results. Prokaryotic System Based kits, typically involving bacterial extracts, are efficient and cost-effective but may have limitations in post-translational modifications. In Vitro Cell Extracts Based kits provide a high degree of control and customizability for protein synthesis, making them suitable for complex proteins, though they may be more expensive and resource-intensive.</p></p>
