<p><p>The commercial laundry machine market comprises various types, including tunnel washers and washer extractors. Washer extractors, on the other hand, are versatile machines that combine washing and extracting functions, offering flexibility for smaller or medium-sized laundries. Both types are crucial for industries requiring high efficiency and reliability in textile care.</p></p>
