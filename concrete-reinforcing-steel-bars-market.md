<p><ul><li>Hot Rolled Deformed Bars.</li><li>Cold Rolled Steel Bars.</li><li>Mild Steel Bars</li><li>Twisted Steel Bars</li><li>Welded Steel Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete reinforcing steel bars market comprises various types tailored for specific structural applications. Hot rolled deformed bars feature surface profiles that enhance bonding with concrete, offering high tensile strength. Cold rolled steel bars are produced through a process that improves strength and dimensional accuracy. Mild steel bars provide flexibility and ductility, making them suitable for various construction needs. Twisted steel bars are designed for better adhesion and strength. Welded steel wire, often used in mesh form, offers uniform tensile strength for reinforced concrete applications.</p></p>
