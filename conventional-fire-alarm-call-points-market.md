<p><ul><li>Glass Element</li><li>Plastic Element</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conventional fire alarm call points market can be categorized into two main types: glass element and plastic element call points. Glass element call points consist of a breakable glass panel that is activated when broken, providing a clear indication of use, suitable for high-traffic areas. Conversely, plastic element call points utilize a durable plastic design with a push-button mechanism for activation, offering greater resistance to vandalism. Both types are essential for fire safety, ensuring quick response in emergencies.</p></p>
