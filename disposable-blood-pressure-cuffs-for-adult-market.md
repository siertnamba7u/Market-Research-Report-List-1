<p><p>The disposable blood pressure cuff market for adults is categorized into one-tube and two-tube designs. One-tube disposable cuffs feature a single air inflation channel, simplifying usage and reducing costs, making them ideal for low-resource settings. In contrast, two-tube disposable cuffs have separate channels for inflation and deflation, allowing for more accurate readings and faster pressure release, which enhances patient comfort and monitoring efficiency. Both types offer hygiene benefits by eliminating the risk of cross-contamination.</p></p>
