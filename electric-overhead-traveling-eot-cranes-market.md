<p><p>Electric Overhead Traveling (EOT) cranes are categorized into several types based on design and application. Single girder cranes utilize one main beam, offering a lighter and cost-effective option for lower capacities. Double girder cranes feature two beams, providing enhanced strength and lifting capabilities for heavier loads and larger spans. Gantry cranes are mobile systems with a frame supported by legs, suitable for outdoor use. Monorails consist of a single track for efficient material handling along a defined path, ideal for specific workflows.</p></p>
