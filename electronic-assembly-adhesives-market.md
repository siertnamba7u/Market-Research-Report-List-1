<p><p>The electronic assembly adhesives market is divided into two main types: electrically conductive and non-electrically conductive adhesives. Electrically conductive adhesives are used to create electrical connections between components, essential for circuit integrity in a variety of electronic devices. In contrast, non-electrically conductive adhesives primarily serve as bonding agents that secure components without affecting electrical conductivity. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring durability, reliability, and performance in electronic assemblies, catering to different application needs in industries such as consumer electronics and automotive.</p></p>
