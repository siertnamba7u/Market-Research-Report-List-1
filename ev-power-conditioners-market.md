<p><p>The EV Power Conditioners market encompasses two primary types: V2H (Vehicle-to-Home) and V2G (Vehicle-to-Grid) Power Conditioners. V2H systems enable electric vehicles to supply power to a home, enhancing energy management and providing backup during outages. V2G technologies, on the other hand, allow EVs to feed electricity back into the grid, facilitating energy storage solutions and stabilizing grid demand. Both types are integral to advancing renewable energy use and optimizing energy consumption in smart grids.</p></p>
