<p><p>The Face Recognition Attendance System market comprises two main types: 2D and 3D face recognition technologies. </p><p>2D face recognition uses standard cameras to capture images, relying on facial features like contours and measurements from a frontal viewpoint. In contrast, 3D face recognition utilizes advanced technology to create a three-dimensional representation of the face, offering enhanced accuracy and the ability to capture depth and structure, making it less susceptible to alterations in lighting or angles. Both types aim to streamline attendance tracking and enhance security.</p></p>
