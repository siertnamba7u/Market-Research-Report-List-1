<p><strong>The Fingerprint Door Access System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical</li><li>Capacitive</li><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fingerprint door access system market can be categorized into several types, including optical, capacitive, ultrasonic, and others. Optical systems capture fingerprints through light, using sensors to create high-resolution images. Capacitive systems rely on electrical impulses to detect the unique patterns of ridges and valleys on a finger. Ultrasonic systems utilize sound waves to create a three-dimensional image of the fingerprint. Additionally, "others" may encompass hybrid technologies or alternative biometric methods, expanding the versatility of access solutions.</p></p>
