<p><p>The Fingerprint Mobile Biometrics market comprises two primary types: Non-AFIS and AFIS technology. Non-AFIS technology focuses on mobile applications that utilize fingerprint recognition for authentication purposes, typically on personal devices without centralized databases. In contrast, AFIS (Automated Fingerprint Identification System) technology involves large-scale databases for matching fingerprints and is often used by law enforcement and government agencies. Both technologies enhance security and streamline identification processes in various sectors, including banking, healthcare, and law enforcement.</p></p>
