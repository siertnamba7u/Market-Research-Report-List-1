<p><p>The Front and Rear Air-Conditioning (AC) Thermal Systems market features two primary types: open loop and closed loop systems. An open loop system draws outside air, allowing for direct ventilation but may be less efficient in extreme temperatures. In contrast, a closed loop system recirculates internal air, providing better temperature control and energy efficiency. Both systems serve diverse applications, from passenger vehicles to heavy-duty trucks, catering to different climate management needs and energy consumption preferences.</p></p>
