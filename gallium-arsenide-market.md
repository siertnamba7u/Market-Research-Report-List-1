<p><p>The Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) market can be categorized into LEC (Lateral Epitaxial over a Substrate) grown GaAs, VGF (Vertical Gradient Freeze) grown GaAs, and other methods. LEC grown GaAs is known for its high purity and efficiency, making it suitable for high-performance applications like photovoltaics and RF devices. VGF grown GaAs offers better thermal stability and uniformity, often used in optoelectronics. Other methods may include MBE (Molecular Beam Epitaxy) and MOCVD (Metal-Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition), catering to specialized applications.</p></p>
