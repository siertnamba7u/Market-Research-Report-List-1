<p><strong>The GuitarPick Up Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sound Hole Pickup</li><li>Piezoelectric Pickup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The guitar pickup market consists mainly of sound hole pickups and piezoelectric pickups. Sound hole pickups are typically used in acoustic guitars and are designed to amplify sound directly from the instrument's body, allowing for a warm, rich tone. In contrast, piezoelectric pickups capture vibrations from the strings and the instrument's body through piezoelectric materials, offering a more direct and crisp sound. Both types cater to different musical styles and preferences, enhancing the versatility of guitar performances.</p></p>
