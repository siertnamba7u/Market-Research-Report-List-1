<p><p>The hot melt hose market includes three main types: PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), PE (Polyethylene), and metal-lined plastic composite hoses. PTFE hoses offer excellent chemical resistance and high-temperature tolerance, making them suitable for demanding applications. PE hoses are lightweight and flexible, ideal for lower-temperature uses. Metal-lined plastic composite hoses combine the durability of metal with the lightweight properties of plastics, providing enhanced flexibility and resistance to wear and abrasion. Each type is designed for specific industrial needs, ensuring efficient hot melt material transfer.</p></p>
