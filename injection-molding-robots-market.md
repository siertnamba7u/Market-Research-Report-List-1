<p><p>Injection molding robots are categorized based on their axis configurations, impacting their versatility and application. 4-axis robots primarily handle pick-and-place tasks, suitable for simple automation. 5-axis robots add rotation for more complex operations, enhancing flexibility. 6-axis robots provide advanced maneuverability, enabling intricate tasks and greater reach. 7-axis robots further enhance versatility with additional rotation points, beneficial in tight spaces. Other market types may include specialized robots or hybrid systems designed for unique molding requirements, optimizing production efficiency.</p></p>
