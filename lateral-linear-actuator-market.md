<p><p>The lateral linear actuator market is categorized into three main types: screw-driven actuators, belt drive actuators, and other variants. Screw-driven actuators utilize a screw mechanism for precise linear movement and high load capacities, making them suitable for applications requiring accuracy. Belt drive actuators employ a belt system for smooth and fast motion, ideal for lighter loads and longer travel distances. Other types may include pneumatic and hydraulic actuators, catering to specialized needs in various industrial applications.</p></p>
