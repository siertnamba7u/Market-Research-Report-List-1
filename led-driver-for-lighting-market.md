<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The LED Driver for Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Current LED Drivers</li><li>Constant Voltage LED Drivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LED drivers are essential components in the lighting market, functioning to manage power supply to LED lights. Constant current LED drivers provide a steady current irrespective of voltage fluctuations, ensuring uniform brightness and prolonging lifespan. Conversely, constant voltage LED drivers supply a fixed voltage, allowing for multiple LEDs to be connected in parallel. Each type caters to specific applications, with constant current drivers commonly used in high-power LEDs, while constant voltage drivers are ideal for LED strips and lower-power installations.</p></p>
