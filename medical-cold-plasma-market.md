<p><strong>The Medical Cold Plasma Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Cold Plasma</li><li>Low-pressure Cold Plasma</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical cold plasma market is categorized into two main types: atmospheric cold plasma and low-pressure cold plasma. Atmospheric cold plasma operates at ambient pressure, enabling easy application for surface sterilization, wound healing, and infection control. In contrast, low-pressure cold plasma is generated in a controlled vacuum environment, often used for medical device sterilization and cancer treatment. Both types leverage unique ionized gas properties to promote healing, reduce microbial load, and enhance therapeutic effects in various medical applications.</p></p>
