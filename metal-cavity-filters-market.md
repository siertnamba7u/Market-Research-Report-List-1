<p><p>The metal cavity filters market consists of various types, including bandpass, high pass, low pass, and others. Bandpass filters allow specific frequency ranges to pass while blocking others, making them essential for communication systems. High pass filters enable signals above a certain frequency to pass, useful in audio and RF applications. Low pass filters allow signals below a designated frequency, commonly used to reduce noise. Other types may include notch filters and tunable filters, catering to specialized applications.</p></p>
