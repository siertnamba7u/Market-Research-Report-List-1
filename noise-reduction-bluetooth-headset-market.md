<p><ul><li>Active Noise Reduction</li><li>Passive Noise Reduction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The noise reduction Bluetooth headset market is primarily divided into two types: active noise reduction (ANR) and passive noise reduction (PNR). ANR technology uses microphones and speakers to detect and counteract ambient noise by generating sound waves that cancel it out, providing an immersive listening experience. In contrast, PNR relies on physical design elements, such as ear cups and padding, to block external sounds by creating a seal around the ears, offering basic noise isolation without electronic intervention.</p></p>
