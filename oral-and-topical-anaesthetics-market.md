<p><strong>The Oral and Topical Anaesthetics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aqueous</li><li>Non-aqueous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oral and topical anesthetics market can be categorized into two main types: aqueous and non-aqueous formulations. Aqueous anesthetics are water-based solutions that facilitate quick absorption and onset of action, often used for procedures requiring rapid relief of pain. Non-aqueous anesthetics, on the other hand, are oil-based or viscous gels, providing longer-lasting effects and better adherence to mucosal surfaces. Both types serve essential roles in dentistry, dermatology, and minor surgical procedures, catering to different patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
