<p><p>The Ovulation Predictor Kit (OPK) market is primarily divided into two types: urine-based and saliva-based kits. Urine-based OPKs detect the surge of luteinizing hormone (LH) in urine, indicating impending ovulation, and are widely popular for their ease of use and accuracy. Saliva-based OPKs analyze changes in the salt concentration in saliva, which reflects hormonal fluctuations. While urine-based kits are more prevalent due to their reliability, saliva-based options appeal to those seeking a reusable and less intrusive alternative.</p></p>
