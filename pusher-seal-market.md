<p><ul><li>Single Coil Spring Seal</li><li>Multiple Spring Seals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pusher seal markets are categorized into single coil spring seals and multiple spring seals based on their construction and application. Single coil spring seals utilize a single coil to maintain contact, ideally suited for low-pressure and temperature applications. In contrast, multiple spring seals consist of several springs, providing enhanced stability and adaptability in high-pressure or fluctuating conditions. Both types are essential in ensuring effective sealing in various industrial environments, minimizing leakage and ensuring operational efficiency.</p></p>
