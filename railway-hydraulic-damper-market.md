<p><ul><li>Primary & Secondary Suspension (Horizontal & Vertical)</li><li>Yaw Dampers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway hydraulic damper market comprises two main types: primary and secondary suspension systems. Primary suspension dampers are crucial for absorbing vertical shocks and maintaining ride comfort by dampening oscillations between the wheelset and the rail. Secondary suspension dampers enhance lateral stability and track alignment, addressing horizontal movements. Yaw dampers, which prevent unwanted rotational motion, play a critical role in enhancing train stability and safety. Together, these dampers optimize overall train performance and passenger comfort in rail systems.</p></p>
