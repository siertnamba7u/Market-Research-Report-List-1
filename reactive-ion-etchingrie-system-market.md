<p><p>A Reactive Ion Etching (RIE) system is a sophisticated technique used in semiconductor manufacturing and microfabrication processes. It combines physical and chemical etching to achieve precise material removal, enabling the production of intricate patterns on silicon wafers and other substrates. The process involves subjecting the material to ionized gases, where reactive ions interact with the surface, enhancing etching selectivity and feature resolution.</p><p>The RIE system market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing demand in various sectors such as electronics, telecommunications, and nanotechnology.
