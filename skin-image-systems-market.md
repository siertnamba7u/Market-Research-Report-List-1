<p><ul><li>Ultrasound Based Skin Imaging Systems</li><li>Optical Based Skin Imaging Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Skin Imaging Systems market comprises two primary types: Ultrasound-Based Skin Imaging Systems and Optical-Based Skin Imaging Systems. Ultrasound systems utilize high-frequency sound waves to capture detailed images of skin layers, aiding in the assessment of skin health and conditions. In contrast, Optical-Based Systems employ light wavelengths to analyze skin properties, offering non-invasive insights into pigmentation, texture, and vascularity. Both technologies play crucial roles in dermatology, enabling accurate diagnosis and effective treatment planning for various skin disorders.</p></p>
