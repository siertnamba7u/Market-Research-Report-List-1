<p><ul><li>Full Embedding Stimulator</li><li>Semi-embedding Stimulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spinal stimulator market comprises two main types: full embedding stimulators and semi-embedding stimulators. Full embedding stimulators are fully implanted within the body, offering a permanent solution for chronic pain management, with minimal external components. In contrast, semi-embedding stimulators feature partially external components, allowing for easier adjustments and potential retrieval if needed. Both types aim to alleviate pain by disrupting pain signals to the brain, catering to diverse patient needs and preferences in pain management strategies.</p></p>
