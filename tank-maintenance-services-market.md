<p><p>The tank maintenance services market is divided into two main types: Aboveground Storage Tanks (AST) and Underground Storage Tanks (UST). ASTs, which are typically used for storing liquids, necessitate regular inspections, cleaning, and repairs to prevent leaks and ensure safety. In contrast, USTs, buried beneath the surface, face challenges like corrosion and are often subject to strict regulatory requirements. Maintenance services for USTs involve monitoring for leaks, environmental protection, and ongoing inspections to safeguard groundwater resources.</p></p>
