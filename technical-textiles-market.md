<p><p>The technical textiles market can be categorized into three main types: nonwovens, composite textiles, and traditional textiles. Nonwovens are engineered fabrics made from fibers bonded together without weaving, ideal for applications like hygiene products and filtration. Composite textiles combine different materials to enhance properties such as strength and durability, commonly used in automotive and aerospace industries. Traditional textiles, while primarily focused on aesthetics, also find applications in technical areas, leveraging advancements in fiber technology for performance enhancement.</p></p>
