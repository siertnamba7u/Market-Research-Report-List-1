<p><p>The tool joint market is categorized into three primary types: threaded screw-on type, counter-bore weld type, and flash or inertia weld type. The threaded screw-on type features a classic design allowing easy disassembly and repair. Counter-bore weld type offers enhanced structural integrity through welding, making it suitable for high-stress applications. Flash or inertia weld type provides a rapid and robust joining method, ensuring excellent strength. Each type serves specific industry needs based on performance, ease of maintenance, and manufacturing efficiency.</p></p>
