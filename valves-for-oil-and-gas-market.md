<p><p>The oil and gas market features various valve types essential for controlling fluid flow. Gate valves provide unobstructed flow and are suited for on/off applications. Control valves regulate flow by adjusting the opening, ensuring precise management. Ball valves offer quick shut-off and reliability, while butterfly valves are space-efficient and ideal for large volumes. Plug valves, with their simple design, excel in on/off control. Each valve type plays a critical role in ensuring efficient operations across the industry.</p></p>
