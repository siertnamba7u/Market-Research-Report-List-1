<p><p>The VOC Concentration Rotor market is primarily divided into two types: Zeolite and Activated Carbon. Zeolite rotors utilize a mineral-based structure that effectively adsorbs volatile organic compounds (VOCs) due to its high surface area and selectivity for specific molecules. Activated Carbon rotors, on the other hand, rely on carbon's porous nature to trap VOCs, offering a broad adsorption capacity. Both types are crucial in air pollution control and industrial processes, each providing distinct advantages in VOC removal efficiency.</p></p>
