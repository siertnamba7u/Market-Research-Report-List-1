<p><strong>The Woodworking Blade Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>T-Shank</li><li>U-Shank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The woodworking blade market features two primary types: T-Shank and U-Shank blades. T-Shank blades are designed with a tang that fits into the tool's clamp, providing a secure grip and making them ideal for jigsaws. They offer better stability and are commonly used for intricate cuts. U-Shank blades, on the other hand, have a U-shaped top that allows for easy insertion into tool holders but may not offer the same level of secure fit as T-Shank blades. Both types cater to different woodworking needs.</p></p>
